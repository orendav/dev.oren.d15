## MENU SYSTEM - DETAILED ANALYSIS

### Hamburger Menu (Mobile Drawer)
- Opens as a **left-side slide-out drawer** overlaying the page
- Menu items listed vertically:
  1. **Shop By Categories** (with right arrow > indicating submenu)
  2. **Shop By Room** (with right arrow >)
  3. **Tables & Desks** (with right arrow >)
  4. **Chairs & Stools** (with right arrow >)
  5. **Pages** (with right arrow >)
  6. **Theme Features** (with right arrow >)
  7. **On Sale** (red/accent colored text, no arrow)
- Bottom section:
  - Find a store (map pin icon)
  - Sign in/Register (person icon)
  - United States (USD $) (expandable country selector)
  - Social media icons row: X, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube

### "Shop By Categories" Mega Menu (Type: promotion_banner)
**Style: Multi-column text links with promotional image**

Layout is a full-width dropdown containing:
- **4 text link columns** on the left (~60% width):
  1. **Furniture** (bold heading link): Sofas, Tables & Desks, Chair & Stools, Shelves, Shop All
  2. **Table & Desks** (bold heading link): Dining Tables, Coffee Table, Side Tables, Work Desk, Dining Tables, Table Lamps, Shop All
  3. **Chair & Stools** (bold heading link): Dining Chairs, Bar Stools, Office Chairs, Arm Chairs, Shop All
  4. **Home & Decor** (bold heading link): Rugs, Candles, Wall Decors, Mirrors, Ottomans, Floor Lamps, Pillows, Shop All
- **Large promotional image** in the center-right area (lifestyle photo of red shelving unit with lamp)
- **Promotional card** on the far right: "Home & Decor / Decoration From $10 / Shop Now" with orange/red chair image

### "Shop By Room" Mega Menu (Type: sidebar)
**Style: Sidebar navigation with image grid -- DIFFERENT from Categories**

Layout is a full-width dropdown with a fundamentally different design:
- **Left sidebar** (~15% width): Vertical list of room names as clickable tabs
  - Living Room (active/bold, with > chevron)
  - Dining & Kitchen (with > chevron)
  - Bed Room (with > chevron)
  - Work Room (with > chevron)
  - Outdoor (with > chevron)
- **Right content area** (~85% width): 2x4 grid of **collection image cards**
  - Each card has: Square lifestyle photo + text label below
  - Living Room tab shows: Tables & Desks, Sofas, Chairs & Stools, Storages & Cabinets, Bow Chairs, Cross Bar Chairs, Single Coffee Tables, Soft Bench
  - Clicking a room tab changes the content on the right (different subcategories per room)

**Key difference:** Categories uses text-only columns; Room uses an image-based grid with tab-switching sidebar.

### "Tables & Desks" Mega Menu (Type: product_list)
**Style: Sidebar category list + product cards**

- **Left sidebar**: Vertical list of subcategories:
  - Tables, Side Tables, Passage Lounge Table, Over Table, Dining Tables, Harbour Table, Umanoff Side Table
- **Right content**: Scrollable product cards with images, prices, badges (Sale, Selling Fast, etc.)
  - Example: Swivel Table $305.00 ~~$345.00~~

### "Chairs & Stools" Mega Menu (Type: product_list)
**Style: Same product_list style as Tables & Desks**

- **Left sidebar**: Chairs, Dining Chairs, Office Chairs, Accent Chairs, Lounge Chairs, Outdoor Chairs, Counter Chairs
- **Right content**: Product cards with images, "New"/"Sale" badges, prices
  - Example: Botani Mist $65.00 (New badge)

### Search Bar / "All Categories" Dropdown
- The search bar has a built-in **category filter dropdown** (HTML `<select>` combobox)
- Default: "All Categories"
- Available categories: Accessories, Armchairs, Bar Stools, Bowls, Candles, Chairs, Desks, Floating Wall, Lamps, Ottomans, Pillows, Planters, Rack Wall, Rugs, Sofa, Sofas, Tables, Towels, Vases
- Search input: "What are you looking for?"
- Rounded pill shape search bar with grey background
- Native browser dropdown (not custom styled overlay)

### "On Sale" Navigation Item
- Special styling: Red text color (#C4301C)
- Animated star/sparkle icon images
- Links directly to /collections/sale-items-v2 (no dropdown)
