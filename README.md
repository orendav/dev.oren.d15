# wholesale-blank-tshirts.com.au

New e-commerce website for an Australian wholesale blank t-shirt supplier.

---

## Design Inspiration

### 1. [Leo Shield](https://www.leoshield.com.au/) — Wholesale Workwear & PPE

**Platform:** Shopify (Softali theme)

**What to take from it:**

- **Announcement bar** — rotating messages (free shipping threshold, same-day dispatch, warehouse count, secure checkout)
- **Banner collage hero** — visual grid layout showcasing key categories instead of a single slider
- **Trust/value strip** — icon row below hero: Same Day Dispatch, Express Delivery, Secure Payment
- **Product cards** — thumbnail hover-swap (second image on hover), quick-view + add-to-cart CTAs
- **Popular categories** — tag-style grid linking to 12+ categories
- **Brand showcase** — logo strip for supplier credibility (60+ brands)
- **Testimonials carousel** — real customer quotes across industry verticals
- **Business account CTA** — prominent "Apply for a Business Account" for wholesale pricing
- **Color palette** — navy (#142c73), white, light grey/blue accents, gold (#ffd701) highlights
- **Typography** — Figtree sans-serif, light weight, clean and modern
- **Footer** — multi-column: warehouse locations, full category links, policies, newsletter, ABN

---

### 2. [Top Banana](https://topbanana.com.au/) — B2B/B2C Office Supplies

**Platform:** Shopify-based e-commerce

**What to take from it:**

- **Multi-level nav** — dropdowns organized by product category AND by industry vertical
- **Hero carousel** — full-width rotating banners with strong taglines and contrasting orange CTAs
- **Bulk pricing** — tiered pricing dropdowns directly on product cards
- **Grid/list toggle** — lets buyers switch between views on collection pages
- **Category tiles** — visual homepage grid linking to top categories + top sellers
- **Brand logo showcase** — builds trust with recognized supplier logos
- **Exit-intent popup** — price-match/discount offer on exit
- **Live chat** — Tawk widget for instant support
- **Loyalty/rewards program** — repeat buyer incentive
- **WhatsApp contact** — low-friction communication channel
- **Color palette** — orange (#FF9800) primary, white backgrounds, dark blue accents
- **Footer** — regional office locations (5 cities), policies, contact details

---

### 3. [A Class Building Materials](https://aclassbuildingmaterials.com.au/) — Wholesale Building Supplies

**Platform:** Shopify (Xtra theme)

**What to take from it:**

- **Marquee announcement bar** — scrolling text on dark background for promotions
- **Mega-menu navigation** — image-rich dropdowns with thumbnail icons (30x30) beside subcategory links, multi-column flyouts, uppercase labels
- **Hero slideshow** — full-width auto-rotating with separate mobile/desktop images and subtle dark overlay
- **USP icon bar** — four blocks: High-Quality Products, Competitive Pricing, Extensive Range, Expert Support
- **Collection grids** — thumbnail cards (240px) per category with "View all" + chevron
- **Rich text sections** — descriptive content blocks with H2/H3 headings for SEO
- **Quick-link grids** — 14+ product category cards for fast navigation
- **Trade credit application** — prominent link for wholesale buyers to apply
- **Calculator tool** — fencing calculator (we could adapt for bulk t-shirt pricing)
- **Review integrations** — Judge.me and TrustShop for social proof
- **WhatsApp/SMS** — primary contact channels with floating phone button on mobile
- **BNPL options** — Zip, Afterpay, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay in footer
- **Color palette** — dark navy-indigo (#131546), deep purple (#261d51) accents, orange-red (#fc4719) for highlights, white body
- **Typography** — Inter font (400, 600 weights), clean sans-serif
- **Footer** — multi-column with office locations, hours, social icons (IG, YouTube, FB, TikTok), quick links, payment icons

## Common Design Patterns Across All Three

| Pattern | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Shopify platform** | All three run on Shopify — proven for AU wholesale e-commerce |
| **Announcement bar** | Rotating/scrolling promos (free shipping thresholds, dispatch info) |
| **Trust indicators** | Icon strips for shipping, payment security, dispatch speed |
| **Mega-menu navigation** | Category-based with images/icons, multi-column |
| **Business/trade accounts** | Wholesale pricing behind account applications |
| **Multiple contact channels** | Phone, WhatsApp, SMS, live chat |
| **Brand/supplier logos** | Logo strips for credibility |
| **BNPL & payment variety** | Afterpay, Zip, Apple Pay, etc. |
| **Multi-warehouse/location** | Footer shows multiple AU locations |
| **Newsletter signup** | Email capture in footer |
| **Mobile-first patterns** | Sticky nav, floating contact buttons, responsive heroes |
